Introducing the new University Complaint Portal

This web application is developed by the university students for all of the residents of the university. The primary objective of this tool is to enable the residents of the university to have their concerns about the campus heard by the administration through a proper channel. This application will allow the residents of the university to lodge their complaints and enable them to view the status of their complaints also. This will also enable the administrative authority to work more efficiently and effectively in solving complaints
